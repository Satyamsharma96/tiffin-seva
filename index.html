Meet Our Founder

The vision behind Tiffin Seva

Satyam Sharma

Founder & CEO, Tiffin Seva

With a passion for home-cooked food and a vision to make healthy meals accessible to everyone, Satyam Sharma founded Tiffin Seva in 2025. His mission is to bridge the gap between homemade taste and office convenience.

Having experienced the challenges of finding quality tiffin services himself, Satyam designed Tiffin Seva with a focus on fresh ingredients, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction. His hands-on approach ensures that every meal meets the highest standards of quality and taste.

Under his leadership, Tiffin Seva has grown to serve multiple areas in Lucknow with plans to expand across the city and beyond.
